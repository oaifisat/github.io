OPEN AGRICULTURE INITIATIVE - FISAT

Doorway to an efficient farming system....

Open Agriculture Initiative is a project taken up by the Center of Excellence in Robotics and IoT, FISAT inspired from the MIT Media Lab at MIT, Massachusetts. This project aims in achieving a much better farming system by incorporating technologies that enable the production of a healthier and more sustainable food system. Technology is used to provide , protect and maintain optimal growing conditions throughout the development of the crop. In this way, we can achieve optimal use of resources such as water, energy, space, capital and labour.
